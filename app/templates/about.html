About Us

Welcome to King Kayak, your number one source for all things Kayaki. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of Kayaks, with an emphasis on quality, craftmanship, and style.

Founded in 1840 by Prince Kayak, King Kayak has come a long way from its beginnings in Antartica. When Prince Kayak first started out, his passion for escaping the tundra led him to design and craft a strudy, quality kayak that everyone loved. His passion did not end there, after escaping, his love for kayaks drove him to start his own business.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
